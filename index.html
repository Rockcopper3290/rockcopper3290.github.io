Card FU

Card Card Fu is one of my pride and joys. Based off of a childhood concept this rock-paper-scissors style game is simple in design yet has a lot of inbuilt strategy. Card Fu forced me to learn to utilise Scriptable objects which allowed for the easy creation of all the different cards that were used in the game.

When Pigs Fly

In a one-day game jam with the prompt "One button game", WPF was born. It essentially boils down to a vertical Flappy Bird but acted as a proof of concept for if I'd be good at game jams. WPF taught me to lower my overall scope for the project/refine the scope to fit the deadline. As WPF was made in a one-day Game Jam, it forced me to limit the expected scope of the game to allow for a successful project.

When the dust settles

A game built in uni with the intention of making a game that was less based on skill and more on environmental storytelling. The main challenge faced with this project was the fact that we had to use Unreal Engine 5 (none of us in the group had ever worked in Unreal before). This forced us to adapt our planning and scope to allow for the inclusion of researching how Unreal works and how to best utilise it to create a stunning work of art.

Swing Shot

Swing Shot is a Work-in-progress game built in a group for our Uni final Project. As of now, we have 1 fully laid out level and all fundamental gameplay components built up already to move into the final stages of production. Communication has been a key factor in the development of Swing Shot as we have many people completing different tasks for very opposite sides of the game, from art to level design and to programing just to name a few.
